🍫 Chocolate Cake

  • 1½ cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • ½ cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 cup milk
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C.
  2. Mix all dry ingredients.
  3. Add milk, oil, and vanilla.
  4. Pour batter into a greased pan.
  5. Bake for 30–35 minutes.
  6. Let it cool and serve!

Prep Time: 15 mins

Cook Time: 35 mins

Serves: 6
